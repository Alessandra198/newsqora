How it works

You bring what you saw. She reads the sources.

1

Bring a claim, link, or screenshot

Paste a claim, share a link, or capture a screenshot. Text inside images is read on your device; the image itself is never uploaded.

2

She reads real sources

Newsqora consults verifiable sources and weighs what they actually say: who reported it, when, and where, without inserting an opinion.

3

Graded confidence, fully cited

You receive a confidence grade with every source listed and linked, so you can check the reasoning yourself rather than take it on faith.

The grade is never binary

Why trust it

Built to be checked, not believed.

Sourced

Every answer is cited

Nothing is asserted without a source behind it. Each citation names who said it, when, and where, with a link to follow.

Neutral

No opinions, no positions

Newsqora gives no opinions and takes no political or religious positions. Contested topics are presented from each side with equal weight.

Private

Your work, kept for you

Beta access uses Google sign-in so your investigations save to folders, your history syncs across devices, and support is possible. Screenshots are read on your device and never uploaded; only the claim you choose to check is sent for analysis, in the EU.

Honest

Admits its limits

When verified sources are insufficient, she says so plainly rather than guessing or filling the gap with invention.

Perspectives

Contested topics, shown with equal weight.

On questions where reasonable sources disagree, Newsqora sets the perspectives side by side, each with its own citations, neither favoured, and takes no position of her own.
